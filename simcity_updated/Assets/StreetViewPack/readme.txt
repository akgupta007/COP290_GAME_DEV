Collection of Streetview images for backgrounds.

Use Streetview converter (https://www.assetstore.unity3d.com/en/#!/content/28512) to convert any Streetview panorama into texture.
 
How to use

- right-click Asset tab and select Create/Legacy/Cubemap
- select Face size 1024
- define images for 6 sides (+Z - side1_..., -Z - side2_..., +Y - side3_..., -Y - side4_..., +X - side5_..., -X - side6_...,)
- right-click Asset tab and choose Create/Material
- assign Material shader StreetViewConverter/StreetView
- assign Material cubemap
- add big sphere to scene
- assign Material to sphere
- place your camera into sphere
